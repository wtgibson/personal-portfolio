About


Growing up in a household headed by two entrepreneurs, I was instilled with a passion for business at a very young age. To me, business is an art form that presents the challenge of consistently providing customers meaningful products and services while contemplating changing financial, technical, and regulatory constraints.

Through my prior work experience, I have garnered an understanding of capital markets and revenue/cost management, as well as an expertise in financial modeling, data analysis/visualization, and the presentation of key insights. All of my involvement, both in the business world and outside of it, has shaped me into the organized, persistent, and resourceful self-starter that I am today.

Inspired by innovation at the intersection of technology and business, as well as passionate about all things entrepreneurship, investing, and personal finance. Feel free to reach out and connect with me!
